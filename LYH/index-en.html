About

Hello! I'm Liu, Yi-Hsien from Taoyuan, Taiwan, a punctual and easy-going person. In my free time, I enjoy watching dramas, listening to music, attending concerts, and collecting postcards.

I have a multidisciplinary background. After graduating from the Applied Foreign Languages Department of National Taipei University of Business, I continued my studies in the Department of Statistics and Information Science at Fu Jen Catholic University. I have earned two foreign language certifications, including a TOEIC score of 845 and a TOPIK Level 5, as well as a Google Analytics certification, which reflect my dedication and achievements in foreign languages and statistics.

Additionally, from October 2024 to January 2025, I participated in the Ministry of Labor's Industry New Talents program, "Practical Training on Interactive Front-End Web Design and Development (7th Session)," where I learned to use Figma for UI/UX design and gained skills in front-end programming (HTML, CSS, JavaScript).
